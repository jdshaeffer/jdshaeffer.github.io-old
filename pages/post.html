The Gear Calculator - An iOS App

I learned a lot on my Appalachian Trail hike. A huge concern for thru-hikers is to keep their packs as light as possible. In order to alleviate the time-consuming activities of gear research and weighing, I created The Gear Calculator to let hikers see a huge selection of gear and its data all in one place.
